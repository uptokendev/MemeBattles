## Trading (buy/sell)
- 2% buy, 2% sell
- from each trade amount T:
  - League Treasury: 0.0075 × T
  - Protocol revenue: 0.0125 × T

## UpVotes
- 0.003 BNB per UpVote → protocol revenue

## Graduation
- 2% finalize fee → protocol revenue
- remainder split:
  - 80% liquidity
  - 20% creator payout
