## Fee table
| Fee | Amount | Paid when | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Trading fee (buy) | 2% | Every buy | Protocol + league funding |
| Trading fee (sell) | 2% | Every sell | Protocol + league funding |
| League funding | 0.75% | Every buy/sell | League prize pools |
| UpVote | 0.003 BNB | When UpVoting | Paid discovery |
| Finalize fee | 2% of raised | At graduation | Protocol revenue |

- The 0.75% is taken from trade notional and routed to League Treasury.
- The remaining portion of the 2% trade fee goes to protocol revenue.
