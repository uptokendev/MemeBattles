# Why we built MemeWarzone

Many meme campaign markets optimize for platform extraction:

- creators pay to launch, pay to promote, and still get buried
- traders get short-term hype cycles and no long-term game loop
- platforms take fees, but value does not flow back to the people who make the ecosystem valuable

We built MemeWarzone around a simple idea:

> Creators, traders, recruiters, and communities should be part of the system's upside.

## 1. Fix discovery

Discovery often turns into pay-to-play shilling.

MemeWarzone replaces hidden boosts with UpVotes, a clear way to show support and push campaigns up the rankings.

## 2. Make campaigns worth returning to

Leagues turn trading and creator performance into weekly and monthly competition.

Winners get rewarded. Campaigns get spotlighted. The ecosystem stays alive.

## 3. Add progression and growth systems

Profiles, rankings, recruiters, squads, and public leaderboards give users a reason to keep building.

Users are not just anonymous flow. They can build status and compound their presence inside the ecosystem.

## 4. Keep money flow transparent

TreasuryRouter routes fees into reward buckets and protocol revenue.

The treasury model then separates protocol revenue, Owners Safe policy, and Ops Safe execution.
