## Fees
- Trading: 2% buy / 2% sell, with 0.75% of trade to League Treasury
- UpVote: 0.003 BNB
- Graduation: 2% finalize fee, then 80/20 split (LP/creator)

## Troubleshooting
- Pending tx: confirm wallet + network, congestion can delay
- Stats lag: indexer may lag during heavy activity
- Missing campaign: verify network + official app link
