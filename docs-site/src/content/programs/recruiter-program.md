The **Recruiter Program** is MemeWarzone's growth layer for people who can bring strong creators, traders, and communities into the ecosystem.

Instead of relying on random shilling, recruiters build a real squad and participate in the value they help create.

## What a recruiter does
A recruiter brings users into MemeWarzone and connects them through a personal recruiter code.

That code can be used by:
- **Creators**
- **Traders**

Once linked, their activity is attributed to the recruiter.

## Recruiter earnings
Recruiters earn from the ecosystem activity connected to them.

### Trading fee share
Recruiters earn **0.25%** on buys and sells generated by linked creators and linked traders.

### Graduation fee share
Recruiters earn **0.30%** when a linked creator's campaign graduates.

This means recruiters are rewarded for bringing in users who actually create activity — not just for sending empty traffic.

## Dashboard
Recruiters get a dedicated dashboard where they can monitor how their squad is performing.

Key dashboard views include:
- linked creators and traders inside the recruiter's **squad**
- current ranking / leaderboard position
- **earnings before payout**
- **lifetime earnings**
- performance and growth visibility over time

## Why the recruiter model matters
The goal is not to create a spam army.

The goal is to create aligned growth:
- recruiters want strong creators to launch
- recruiters want strong traders to stay active
- the platform benefits from better participation
- creators and traders benefit from more coordinated growth

## Why we use a recruiter code
The recruiter code creates a clean attribution layer.

It makes it clear:
- who brought in the user
- which activity belongs to which recruiter
- how earnings should be tracked before payout

That gives MemeWarzone a transparent and scalable referral structure instead of vague off-platform arrangements.
