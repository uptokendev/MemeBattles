---
title: Glossary
description: The few terms you need to understand the platform.
---

- **Bonding curve**: price changes as buys/sells happen during launch phase
- **Campaign**: a launch page + trading instance
- **Claim**: withdrawing rewards to your wallet
- **Epoch**: fixed league window (weekly/monthly)
- **Finalize**: graduation execution step
- **Finalize fee**: 2% fee taken at graduation before split
- **Graduation**: threshold event at 50 BNB
- **League Treasury**: funded by 0.75% of trade notional
- **Owners Safe**: 2-of-3 multisig treasury
- **Ops Safe**: operations wallet held at ~50 BNB
- **UpVote**: paid visibility boost (0.003 BNB)
