---
title: Glossary
description: The few terms you need to understand the platform.
---

- **Bonding curve**: price changes as buys and sells happen during the launch phase
- **Campaign**: a token launch page plus its trading instance
- **Claim**: withdrawing rewards to your wallet
- **Epoch**: a fixed league window (weekly or monthly)
- **Finalize**: the graduation execution step
- **Finalize fee**: the 2% fee taken at graduation before the split
- **Graduation**: the threshold event at 50 BNB
- **League Treasury**: funded by 0.75% of trade notional
- **Owners Safe**: the 2-of-3 multisig treasury
- **Ops Safe**: the operations wallet held around 50 BNB
- **Prepare Mode**: the pre-launch state where creators, traders, and recruiters can get set up before full launch
- **Rank**: a user's military-style progression status inside MemeWarzone
- **Recruiter**: a user who brings creators and traders into the ecosystem and earns from linked activity
- **Recruiter code**: the unique code used to connect creators and traders to a recruiter
- **Squad**: the set of linked creators and traders attributed to a recruiter
- **UpVote**: a paid visibility boost (0.003 BNB)
