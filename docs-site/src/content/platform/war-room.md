title: War Room Chat
description: How campaign-level chat fits into MemeWarzone’s trading and community experience.
---

The War Room is the campaign-level chat experience.

It is meant to sit close to the trading panel so users can follow community activity, launch reactions, and trader conversation without leaving the campaign page.

## What it is for

The War Room helps users:

- follow live campaign sentiment
- coordinate community attention
- ask basic campaign questions
- react to buys, sells, UpVotes, and graduation pushes
- feel the battlefield moving in real time

## What it is not for

The War Room should not be treated as financial advice or proof of safety.

A busy chat does not mean a campaign is safe.

Users should still check:

- deployer history
- campaign details
- token distribution
- liquidity state
- official links
- suspicious activity indicators

## Signing messages

The intended user experience is simple: users should not need to sign every chat message.

Authentication should feel like a normal live chat after wallet/session access is established.

## Moderation direction

War Room moderation should protect the experience from:

- spam
- phishing links
- impersonation
- malicious contract links
- fake support accounts
- coordinated scam messages

Always verify official campaign links before clicking anything shared in chat.
