Leagues are recurring competition systems inside MemeWarzone.

They are designed to make platform activity visible, competitive, and repeatable.

## Why Leagues exist

Most launchpads have a short attention window.

Leagues create recurring moments:

- weekly battles
- monthly finals
- public winners
- creator bragging rights
- trader competition
- community content
- self-funded prize pools

## How Leagues are funded

From every buy and sell, **0.75% of trade notional** routes to the League Treasury.

As trading volume grows, prize capacity can grow with it.

## Main League categories

MemeWarzone uses five core categories:

- Perfect Run
- Fastest Finish
- Biggest Hit
- Top Earner
- Crowd Favorite

Read: **[League categories](/leagues/categories)**.

## Weekly and monthly cycles

Leagues operate in weekly and monthly epochs.

- Weekly epoch: Monday 00:00 UTC to next Monday 00:00 UTC
- Monthly epoch: 1st 00:00 UTC to next 1st 00:00 UTC

Read: **[Epochs & prizes](/leagues/epochs-and-prizes)**.
