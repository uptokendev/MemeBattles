Leagues turn campaign activity into recurring competition.

Weekly epochs run Monday 00:00 UTC to the next Monday 00:00 UTC. Monthly epochs run from the 1st day of the month at 00:00 UTC to the 1st day of the next month at 00:00 UTC.

League prize pools are funded by 0.75% of every buy and sell routed to LeagueTreasury.
