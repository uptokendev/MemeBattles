---

Prepare Mode is the pre-live phase for MemeWarzone.

It lets creators, recruiters, and early users get ready before campaigns become fully live.

## Creators

Creators can prepare:

- campaign name and ticker
- logo and visual identity
- description
- social links
- launch plan
- community plan

The goal is to enter live deployment with a campaign that looks credible and is ready for attention.

## Recruiters

Recruiters can prepare:

- recruiter application
- recruiter code
- share link
- creator pipeline
- trader audience
- squad growth plan

Recruiters should understand attribution, link locking, Squad Pool mechanics, and claim-based payouts before live activity begins.

## Traders

Traders can learn:

- bonding-curve risk
- campaign lifecycle
- League categories
- Warzone BNB Airdrop eligibility
- Squad Pool participation
- official claim safety

## Why Prepare Mode matters

The first live phase will move quickly.

Prepare Mode lets users understand the ecosystem before they connect wallets, deploy campaigns, trade, or chase rewards.
